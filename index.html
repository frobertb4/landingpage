Hiking in Australasia is breathtaking.

Australia, New Zealand, and Southeast Asia is home to mountainous regions with pleasant hikes accompanied by breathtaking views over different landscapes. Sign up to receive our newsletter regarding the most worthwhile hikes you can find in these regions.
Things you will need
Be sure to bring water for hydration, preferably not a single-use bottle.
If it's a sunny day, you will be much better off with some sun protection for the eyes.
Don't forget suncreen, even on a cloudy day. No less than SPF50!
A good pair of shoes will go a long way, especially if you'll be walking on slick rocks.
